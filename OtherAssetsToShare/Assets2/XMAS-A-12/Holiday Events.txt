Holiday events appear during certain holidays and changes certain section (Mostly A-Section) to match the corresponding holiday. With matching decorations, entities and other things. You can also get exclusive items from that event.

You will be able to force certain holidays if you change the date on your device.

The list of Holiday Events (DD/MM)
- Christmas (01/12 - 31/12)
- Halloween (01/10 - 31/10)
- April Fool (01/04 - 12/04)
